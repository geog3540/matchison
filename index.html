Geovisualization Portfolio GEOG 5540

Assignment 3: Choropleth Mapping

A choropleth map of the state of Indiana, representing the percentage of the population infected with Covid-19 (as of February 2021) at the county level.

Assignment 4: Graduated Symbol Mapping

A map of the state of Indiana, with graduated symbols representing the raw population total of individuals aged 65 or older, by county (represented with a basemap).

Assignment 5: Bivariate Choropleth Mapping

A bivariate map of the state of Indiana, comparing the percentage of the population aged 65 or older and the percentage of Covid-19 cases which resulted in death.

Yearly Total Precipitation in Iowa

An isarithmic map of the state of Iowa, created using data from the Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM).

Assignment 7: Small Multiples

A small multiples series of choropleth maps of the state of Illinois, representing the Covid-19 case fatality rate and the rates of three risk factors by county, including histograms. Adjustable by classification method and number of classes.

Environmental Justice Analysis: Project Website

A proposal for the GEOG 5540 term project. The project aims to determine whether there is evidence to suggest that lower-income and/or racial minority communities in the state of Iowa are more likely to be located in close proximity to a solid waste facility or contaminated site.
